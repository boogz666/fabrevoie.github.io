Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 2025

What We Do

We're Fabrevoie, and we make weird shoes. This policy explains how we handle your personal information when you visit our site, buy our products, or join our community.

Information We Collect

When you interact with us, we collect:

  • Contact details (name, email, shipping address)
  • Payment information (processed securely by our payment partners)
  • Order history and preferences
  • Website usage data to improve your experience

How We Use Your Data

Your information helps us:

  • Process and fulfill your orders
  • Send updates about new drops and exclusive releases
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve our website and products
  • Prevent fraud and maintain security

Sharing Your Information

We don't sell your personal data. We only share information with trusted partners who help us operate our business, like payment processors, shipping companies, and email services. These partners are bound by strict confidentiality agreements.

Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies to remember your preferences, analyze site traffic, and provide personalized experiences. You can control cookie settings in your browser, though some features may not work properly if disabled.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we have about you
  • Correct any inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Port your data to another service

Data Security

We take security seriously and use industry-standard measures to protect your information. However, no online system is 100% secure, so we can't guarantee absolute protection.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. When we do, we'll post the changes here and update the effective date. Major changes will be communicated via email.
